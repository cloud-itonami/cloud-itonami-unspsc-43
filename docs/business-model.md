# Business Model: Independent IT Asset Recovery & E-Waste Refurbishment Robotics

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-unspsc-43`
- UNSPSC segment: `43` (Information Technology Broadcasting and
  Telecommunications)
- Activity: IT asset disposition (ITAD) -- device intake, data-wipe
  verification, refurbishment or certified recycling
- Social impact: e-waste reduction, verified data destruction, cheaper
  refurbished device access

## Customer

- businesses decommissioning IT fleets
- schools and nonprofits needing certified data destruction on a budget
- municipalities running e-waste collection programs
- refurbishers who want a governed chain-of-custody tool instead of a
  spreadsheet

## Offer

- device intake and chain-of-custody registration
- automated triage (functional test, data-wipe verification)
- resell / refurbish / certified-recycle disposition recommendation
- sensitive-data or battery-hazard escalation (human sign-off, never
  automated)
- data-destruction certificate and disposition audit export

## Revenue

- per-device processing fee
- certified data-destruction certificate fee
- refurbished-device resale margin
- municipal/enterprise e-waste program contract

## Trust Controls

- disposition recommendations require IT Asset Recovery Governor clearance
- any `:high`/`:safety-critical` finding (unverified sensitive data,
  battery hazard) always escalates to human sign-off before resale or
  recycling
- every triage run, finding and escalation is logged
- public data-destruction/recycling claims must reference measured
  verification evidence
